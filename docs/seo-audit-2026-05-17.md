## Proposed copy edits (awaiting your approval)

Each preserves the lowercase script / warm voice and only adds a single phrase. Apply none, some, or all — reply with the numbers you want.

1. **`components/site/hero.tsx:188-190`** — keyword: `cafe Garchuk`, `cafe near Royal Global University`
   Current:
   > A slow-brewed corner for crisp croissants, warm conversations, and quiet mornings. Doors open soon.
   Proposed:
   > A slow-brewed corner in Garchuk for crisp croissants, warm conversations, and quiet mornings — a short walk from Royal Global University. Doors open soon.
   Rationale: keeps the warm cadence; injects locality + RGU into the only paragraph crawlers will read above the fold.

2. **`components/site/hero.tsx:228`** — keyword: `cafe Garchuk Guwahati`
   Current:
   > Royal Path · Near Royal Global University · Garchuk
   Proposed:
   > A cafe on Royal Path · Near Royal Global University · Garchuk, Guwahati
   Rationale: turns the address strip into a keyword-rich line without losing the typographic dot rhythm.

3. **`components/site/story.tsx:283-285`** — keyword: `cafe Guwahati`
   Current:
   > Small things, done with **great care**.
   Proposed (h2 swap):
   > A small cafe in Guwahati, done with **great care**.
   Rationale: surfaces the "cafe Guwahati" exact-match phrase in an H2 without rewriting the brand line — keeps the script accent on "great care".

4. **`components/site/peek-menu.tsx:80-83`** — keyword: `best cafe Guwahati` (soft form), `brunch Guwahati`
   Current:
   > What's on the **chalkboard**.
   Proposed (subhead under h2):
   > What's on the chalkboard. Slow brunch, specialty coffee, and a few quiet favourites from one of Guwahati's softest new cafes.
   Rationale: gives a body sentence that hits `brunch Guwahati` + frames the brand as a new Guwahati cafe — without making a self-congratulatory "best" claim.

5. **`components/site/surprises.tsx:101-105`** — keyword: `study cafe Guwahati`, `places to work from Guwahati` (long-tail)
   Current:
   > Between now and opening day, we'll be revealing little surprises — recipes, corners of the cafe, and people who made it real. Leave us your email and we'll let you in first.
   Proposed:
   > Between now and opening day, we'll be revealing little surprises — recipes, corners of the cafe, and the quiet two-top by the window we built for people who like to study or work slow in Guwahati. Leave us your email and we'll let you in first.
   Rationale: gentle, in-voice mention of `study` + `work` use-case that captures long-tail "study cafe Guwahati" without keyword-stuffing.

6. **`components/site/visit.tsx:63-67`** — keyword: `cafe near Royal Global University`, `RGU cafe`
   Current:

   Rationale: works in `RGU` abbreviation (people google it short-form) and the "cafe + RGU" pairing in the same sentence.
